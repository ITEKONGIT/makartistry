Our Story

The Art Within

In a world brimming with art and creativity, we believe in weaving the magic of everyday art into your life. Mak Artistry seeks to transform the mundane into extraordinary stories that create lasting memories.

Who says art has to live in a museum? We're bringing it to your doorstep, one creative story at a time.

What We Do

We create art tailored to your style and preferences. Our work spans a wide range, from custom packaging to digital art, ensuring that each piece is uniquely suited to you. We believe in making art a seamless part of your brand and lifestyle.

Why we do it

You know, the world is this incredibly beautiful place, just brimming with art all around us. But with the hustle and bustle of our fast-paced lives, we often miss out on the magic it can bring into our daily routines. That's why I started Mak – to help weave art into our lives, something I fondly call "Everyday Art."

Behind the Brand

A Dynamic Creative Studio that started in 2017

While pursuing her economics studies at the University of Lagos, Mosopefoluwa initiated Mak Artistry—a vibrant creative studio dedicated to transforming art into an engaging daily experience for you, your brand, and your lifestyle.

Her work is inspired by the French minimalism, elegance, and sophistication. Over the years of working with arts and craft, Mosopefoluwa fell in love with making arts. She then decided to start her own studio and has been partnering with businesses of all sizes and industries ever since.
